Services

Petroleum Equipment Company's foundation has always been service. We try to maintain the same integrity of service daily as has been our reputation over the years. We have factory-trained electronic technicians servicing the equipment that we sell. Our shop also has a full staff of mechanics, who service only mechanical dispensers, pumps, meters and related equipment. It is our privilege to serve our customers with the knowledge base that these technicians provide. Our goal is to get your products serviced with as little down time as possible, at the best price possible.

Over the past few years, we have seen many changes occur in our industry. It is our responsibility to stay abreast of these changes, so that we may offer the best service available to our customers. We also have technicians in the Hattiesburg area and Gulf Coast area. Thank you for visiting our web site. Should you need assistance please email me, Blake Walker, at service@petroco.net or call us at 601-932-9100.

Thanks,
Blake Walker, Service Manager
